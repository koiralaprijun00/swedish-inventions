# Lightbringer: Protecting Ideas With AI-First Patent Tools

Lightbringer helps companies defend their intellectual property. Its software searches prior art, generates claim suggestions, and manages prosecution timelines so in-house teams and law firms can move faster with fewer manual spreadsheets.

The company, valued around SEK 250M in late 2024, combines AI classification with expert feedback to keep filings sharp. Investors include Luminar Ventures, Alliance VC, and Zenith Ventures. Clients use Lightbringer to map competitive landscapes and to monitor infringement signals once patents are granted.

For innovators racing to secure rights, Lightbringer is the digital shield that never sleeps.
